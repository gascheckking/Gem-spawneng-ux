The Gamble Rule

Gambling mechanics are ONLY enabled for Fragment and Shard rarities. High-value assets (Core+) are protected from degeneration to ensure economic stability.

Rewards Rail

Every pack open yields 5-10% of value as SpawnCoin/XP. This is minted utility, not a treasury drain. It keeps the loop moving even on "bad" pulls.
